Sushi

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4-5
Ingredients Quantity
Sushi rice 200 g
Nori sheets 4
Chicken 100 g
Cucumber 1 small

Step 1: Cook sushi rice and let it cool.

Step 2: Place a nori sheet on a bamboo mat.

Step 3: Add chicken, cucumber.

Step 4: Roll tightly and slice into pieces.
