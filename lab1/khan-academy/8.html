Travel to South Korea

Things to see in South Korea:

  • Gyeongbokgung Palace in Seoul
  • Jeju Island
  • N Seoul Tower

Places to go:

  1. Busan – famous for beaches and seafood
  2. Incheon – modern city near the sea
  3. Seoul – the capital city full of life

South Korea is an amazing travel destination. It offers unforgettable memories, exciting adventures, and beautiful places to explore.
